## PRELIMS SYLLABUS (common — 8 subjects)
Language (Marathi/English) is NOT in Prelims — it is Mains Paper 1.

1. **History** (`history`)
   - `ancient-medieval` — Indus Valley (basic); Maratha Empire (Shivaji, Swarajya, Ashtapradhan); Peshwa era
   - `modern-india` — Europeans→colonization; British policies (Doctrine of Lapse, Subsidiary Alliance, Ryotwari/Mahalwari); Revolt of 1857; INC 1885 (moderate/extremist); Partition of Bengal 1905 & Swadeshi; Gandhian movements (Champaran, Kheda, NCM, CDM, Quit India); revolutionaries (Chapekar, Savarkar, Bhagat Singh, Azad); Bose & INA; Round Tables; GoI Acts 1909/1919/1935
   - `maharashtra-reformers` — Jyotiba & Savitribai Phule (Satyashodhak Samaj); Ranade (Prarthana Samaj); Agarkar (Sudharak); Tilak (Kesari/Mahratta, Ganeshotsav); Ambedkar (Mahad, Kalaram, Constitution); V.R. Shinde; Karmaveer Bhaurao Patil (Rayat Shikshan); D.K. Karve; Vinoba Bhave; reformers' newspapers (match-the-following); Samyukta Maharashtra Movement (Bapat, Atre, 1 May 1960)
   - `post-independence` — princely states integration (Patel); States Reorganisation Act 1956; Maharashtra & Gujarat 1960

2. **Geography** (`geography`)
   - `maharashtra-geo` — physical divisions (Konkan, Sahyadri, Desh, Vidarbha); peaks/ghats; rivers (Godavari, Krishna, Tapi, Narmada — origin/tributaries/districts); dams; climate/rainfall; soils (regur/laterite/alluvial); forests/sanctuaries/parks; minerals; crops (Kharif/Rabi, cotton/sugarcane); industries (MIDC); admin divisions & districts
   - `india-geo` — physical structure; monsoon/climate; vegetation; rivers (Himalayan/Peninsular); population; agriculture belts/Green Revolution; industrial regions
   - `world-geo` — continents/oceans; lat/long, time zones; earthquakes/volcanoes/cyclones (basic); features in current affairs

3. **Economy** (`economy`)
   - `national-income` — GDP/GNP/NNP/Per Capita; measurement methods
   - `agriculture-eco` — cropping seasons; irrigation; MSP, credit, PMFBY; land reforms
   - `industry-eco` — industrial policy 1948/1956/1991; PSU vs private; Maharashtra industry (Industry Inspector link)
   - `trade-banking` — BoP, export-import; RBI (repo/CRR/SLR); nationalized & cooperative banking (Maharashtra); Jan Dhan
   - `population-social` — census (literacy/sex ratio/urbanization); poverty; unemployment types
   - `govt-finance` — Union/State budget; fiscal/revenue deficit; CAG; GST

4. **Current Affairs** (`current_affairs`)
   - `national-intl` — Union govt policy (6–12 mo); international relations/summits; appointments; awards (Padma/Nobel)
   - `maharashtra-ca` — state GRs & schemes; Maharashtra budget; infra (Samruddhi Mahamarg, Metro, coastal road) [8–10 Qs]
   - `sports-misc` — tournaments/winners (Olympics, Asian Games, Khelo India); books/obituaries; sci-tech (ISRO, defence)

5. **Polity / Governance** (`polity`)
   - `constitution-framework` — Constituent Assembly, architects, sources; Preamble terms; federal/parliamentary features
   - `rights-duties` — Fundamental Rights (Art 12–35); Fundamental Duties (51A); DPSP (Part IV)
   - `union-govt` — President; VP; Parliament (LS/RS); PM & Council
   - `state-govt` — Governor (discretionary powers); CM & Council; Vidhan Sabha & Vidhan Parishad (bicameral)
   - `judiciary` — Supreme Court; High Courts (Bombay HC benches); subordinate judiciary
   - `local-govt` — 73rd Amendment (ZP, Panchayat Samiti, Gram Panchayat); 74th (Corporations, Councils, Nagar Panchayats); State Election Commission

6. **General Science** (`science`)
   - `physics` — motion; Newton's laws; work/energy/power; pressure; light; sound; heat; electricity (Ohm's law); magnetism
   - `chemistry` — atomic structure; periodic table; bonding; acids/bases/salts; reactions; metals/non-metals
   - `biology` — cell (plant vs animal); taxonomy; human body systems; photosynthesis
   - `health-hygiene` — nutrition/vitamins/deficiency; diseases; immunization; public health schemes
   - `sci-tech-dev` — ISRO missions; biotech/IT; environment (pollution, climate change)

7. **Arithmetic (Quantitative Aptitude)** (`arithmetic`)
   - `number-system` — types, divisibility, HCF-LCM; simplification
   - `commercial-maths` — percentage; profit/loss/discount; SI & CI; ratio/proportion/partnership
   - `time-work-speed` — averages; time/speed/distance (trains, boats); time & work (pipes/cisterns)
   - `data-interpretation` — bar/pie/tables/line graphs

8. **Reasoning (Intelligence Test)** (`reasoning`)
   - `series-coding` — number/alphabet series; coding-decoding
   - `relations-direction` — blood relations; direction sense
   - `clock-calendar` — clock angles; calendar day-date
   - `analogy-classification` — analogy; odd-one-out
   - `logical` — syllogisms; statement-conclusion/assumption; seating puzzles; non-verbal (mirror/pattern)

## MAINS SYLLABUS

### Paper 1 — Language (common to all posts, 100 marks)
- **Marathi (50)** — 12th std: vocabulary (समानार्थी/विरुद्धार्थी/शब्दसमूहाला एक शब्द); grammar (संधी, समास, अलंकार, प्रयोग, विभक्ती, काळ, शब्दांच्या जाती); वाक्यरचना/वाक्यशुद्धी; अव्यय; म्हणी व वाक्प्रचार (heavily tested); उतारा (comprehension); translation
- **English (50)** — graduate: vocabulary (syn/antonyms, one-word, homophones); grammar (12 tenses, active-passive, direct-indirect, articles, prepositions, subject-verb agreement, conjunctions); sentence correction/rearrangement; fill-in-blanks; idioms & phrasal verbs; comprehension; common errors

### Paper 2 — General Ability + Subject-Specific (100 marks, POST-SPECIFIC)

**Industry Inspector:**
- A. Current Affairs (national/international, Maharashtra GRs & industrial policy, Make in India, PLI)
- B. Intelligence Test (harder than prelims)
- C. RTI Act 2005 (scope, "information"/"public authority", filing, 30-day/48-hr limits, Sec 8 exemptions, appeals)
- D. Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act 2015 (notified services, timelines, designated/appellate officers, penalties)
- E. Constitution (administrative angle): Centre-State relations (Union/State/Concurrent lists), residuary powers, Governor's ordinance power, State Legislature functioning
- F. Computer & IT (fundamentals, networking LAN/WAN, cybercrime/IT Act 2000, e-governance Aaple Sarkar/RTS/Digital India, MS Office)
- G. **Industrial Policy** (deepest): India policy 1948/1956/1991; Maharashtra incentive schemes; entrepreneurship (Startup/Stand-Up India); industrial clusters; project management/marketing basics; industrial licensing/single-window; MSME schemes (CGTMSE, PMEGP)
- H. **Industrial Acts**: IDR Act 1951; MSMED Act 2006 (Udyam, delayed payment); Facilitation Council; Foreign Trade Policy/EXIM; SEZ Act 2005; Patent Act; Trade & Merchandise Marks Act 1958; Factories Act 1948 (safety/welfare/hours)
- I. Industrial Tribunal & Rehabilitation (sick units/BIFR, NCLT, Industrial Disputes Act basics)
- J. Industrial Development Bodies: MIDC, SICOM, KVIB/KVIC, MPCB, MSSIDC, Govt Store Purchase Policy

**Technical Assistant (Directorate of Insurance):**
- A. General Knowledge (History/Geography/Civics at Mains difficulty)
- B. Current Affairs (national + Maharashtra)
- C. Intelligence Test (harder)
- D. Basic Mathematical & Statistical Skills (speed calc; %, discount; SI/CI; statistics — mean/median/mode, range/SD; data interpretation for insurance/financial data)
- E. RTI Act 2005 (same as above)
- F. Computer & IT (same as above)
- G. **Insurance Concepts** (deepest): evolution/risk-pooling; types (life: term/whole/endowment/ULIP; general: motor/health/fire/marine); social schemes (PMJJBY, PMSBY, Ayushman Bharat); market structure (LIC, GIC, private, intermediaries); terminology (premium, sum assured, underwriting, reinsurance); principles (utmost good faith, insurable interest, indemnity, subrogation, contribution, proximate cause)
- H. **IRDA** (IRDA Act 1999; objectives; functions — licensing, policyholder protection, premium regulation; grievance/Ombudsman)
- I. Directorate of Insurance (Maharashtra): citizens' charter, RTI disclosures, admin structure, state-employee group insurance
